Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Welcome to JobHunter. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name and profile information
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed)

1.2 Resume and Job Application Data

  • Uploaded resumes and CVs
  • Job preferences and search criteria
  • Saved job listings and application status
  • Generated documents (cover letters, tailored resumes)

1.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction data (features used, time spent)
  • Device information (OS version, device type)
  • Crash logs and error reports

1.4 AI Processing Data

  • Resume content processed for AI matching
  • Job description analysis
  • Generated content via AI services

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide job search, resume management, and application tracking features
  • AI Matching: To analyze your resume against job listings and provide match scores
  • Document Generation: To create tailored resumes and cover letters
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications
  • Security: To protect your account and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase — PostgreSQL database with row-level security
  • Encrypted connections — All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication — JWT tokens with secure storage

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • No storage of passwords in plain text

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • OpenAI — AI-powered resume analysis and document generation
  • Adzuna API — Job search data
  • Perplexity API — Enhanced job search capabilities

These services have their own privacy policies and are GDPR/SOC 2 compliant.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Update or correct your information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — Export your data in a standard format
  • Withdraw Consent — Opt-out of AI processing

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion
  • Resumes and documents: Retained until manually deleted by user
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days
  • AI processing logs: Retained for 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

JobHunter is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Compliance

JobHunter complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • App Store Privacy Guidelines
  • Google Play Privacy Policy Requirements
