# Getting Started: Your First Week

Here's a suggested flow to get the most out of JobHunter:

---

## Day 1

Create your account and complete your profile setup.

---

## Day 2

Upload your resume and set it as primary.

---

## Day 3-4

Use Job Search to find 10-15 jobs you're interested in and save them.

---

## Day 5

Generate tailored resumes for your top 3-5 jobs.

---

## Week 2

Set up 2-3 alerts for ongoing monitoring. Start tracking applications in the tracker.

---

## Ongoing

Check your dashboard and analytics weekly to see what's working.

---
