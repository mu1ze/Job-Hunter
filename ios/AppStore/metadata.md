## Description (max 4000 characters)
Job-Hunter is your ultimate career companion, designed to optimize your chances of getting your resume viewed by recruiters. Leveraging intelligent matching and tracking, Job-Hunter analyzes your resume against job descriptions to ensure you pass ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) and catch the hiring manager's eye.

Features:
- **Resume Optimization:** Get actionable feedback to improve your resume's match rate.
- **Job Tracking:** Keep track of your applications, interviews, and offers in one organized dashboard.
- **Deep Match Analysis:** Compare your skills with job requirements instantly.
- **Secure & Private:** Your career data is encrypted and kept private.

Take control of your job search today with Job-Hunter!
